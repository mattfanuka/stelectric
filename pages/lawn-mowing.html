ABOUT THOMAS & SON LANDSCAPING LLC

We are a small, family owned business, proudly serving our community with honest work and reliable care.

At Thomas & Son Landscaping, we believe your property deserves the same care and attention we’d give our own. Based in Vineland and proudly serving Cumberland and Gloucester Counties, we offer a full range of services for both residential and commercial clients. From lawn mowing, mulch installation, gutter cleaning, and leaf removal to snow and ice management, seasonal décor like Christmas light installation, and full landscaping projects — we handle it all.

What sets us apart is our commitment to doing the job right the first time and treating every client like family. With Thomas & Son, you don’t pay until the project is complete (except for materials that require a deposit). No gimmicks, no hidden fees — just dependable service you can count on year-round.
