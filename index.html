Our Services

Electrical Services

Professional electrical installation, repair, and maintenance for residential and commercial properties. From panel upgrades and rewiring to troubleshooting electrical issues, our licensed electricians ensure your property meets all safety codes and operates efficiently.

Lighting

Custom lighting design and installation to enhance both functionality and ambiance. We specialize in LED retrofits, landscape lighting, security lighting, and smart home lighting systems that reduce energy costs while improving visibility and aesthetics.

Signs

Eye-catching commercial signage solutions including illuminated channel letters, monument signs, wayfinding systems, and digital displays. We handle everything from design and permitting to installation and maintenance to maximize your business visibility.

Video Board

Dynamic LED video board installation for sports facilities, entertainment venues, and commercial spaces. High-resolution displays with custom content management systems that engage audiences and deliver impactful messaging in real-time.

Surveys & Permits

Comprehensive electrical surveys, load calculations, and permit acquisition services. We navigate complex building codes and utility requirements to ensure your project meets all regulatory standards and passes inspection on the first try.

Other Specialty Services

Complete property enhancement and maintenance solutions including flag pole installation and maintenance, custom color lighting lenses for distinctive visual effects, specialty welding and fabrication work, sign appearance improvements and refurbishment, professional pressure washing, thorough cleaning services, precision painting, and expert polishing to restore surfaces.
